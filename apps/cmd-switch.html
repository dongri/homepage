CmdSwitch Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2025

Summary: CmdSwitch does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information Collection

CmdSwitch does not collect any personal information or data from its users. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

Data Storage

CmdSwitch only stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Your keyboard input source preferences (which input sources are assigned to left and right Command keys)

This information is stored in your macOS user preferences and never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

CmdSwitch does not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. All operations are performed locally on your device.

Network Activity

CmdSwitch does not connect to the internet and does not transmit any data over the network.

Analytics and Tracking

CmdSwitch does not use any analytics, tracking, or telemetry services.

Permissions

CmdSwitch requires the following macOS permissions to function:

  • Accessibility: Required to monitor Command key presses and switch input sources
  • Input Monitoring: Required to detect keyboard events

These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and no data is collected or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

CmdSwitch does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your Rights

Since CmdSwitch does not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all locally stored preferences.
